Learn Dakuten...and Handakuten

Dakuten and Handakuten are little symbols added to the k,s,t and h rows of the hiragana chart. Dakuten is two small dashes ゛and Handakuten is a small circle ゜. They symbolize a change in the consonant in the pronounciation, so k -> g, s -> z, t -> d and h -> b and p. These changes are illustrated in the table below.

か kaき kiく kuけ keこ ko

が gaぎ giぐ guげ geご go


さ saし shiす suせ seそ so

ざ zaじ jiず zuぜ zeぞ zo


た taち chiつ tsuて teと to

だ daぢ jiづ zuで deど do


は haひ hiふ fuへ heほ ho

ば baび biぶ buべ beぼ bo

ぱ paぴ piぷ puぺ peぽ po
